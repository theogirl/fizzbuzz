The Mini Fizz Buzz Pub App

The Concept:

If you are playing Fizz Buzz while you are drinking in a pub, it may be hard to think straight and do quick mental calculations ("...is 44 evenly divisible by 3? Is that a leprauchan in my beer?" Etc.) So this little app will help you by telling you whether you should just say the number when it's your turn, or if you should yell out "Fizz", "Buzz", or "FizzBuzz" instead.
